Under the Sea

This scratch side-scroller game was created by Bianca and I. Under the Sea is a game
created with the goal of getting the most points in 10 seconds. Peter the person is your
swimmer and you can use the arrows to move him around the screen to collect
as many points as possible in the time limit.
